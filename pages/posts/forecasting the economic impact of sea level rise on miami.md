The objective of this project is to create a simple model that can forecast the economic cost of sea level rise (SLR) on the City of Miami by the year 2150. This report provides an (ideally) engaging introduction to the model, along with secondary analyses not included in the primary model. It is important to point out that this model attempts to forecast cost *in the absence of human intervention*. It seems reasonable to expect this will occur as costs begin to materialize.
